# Customer Demographics & Purchase Behavior Analysis – Retail Analytics

## Project Overview
Understanding customer demographics and purchase behavior is critical for optimizing marketing strategies, inventory planning, and revenue growth.  
This project analyzes **customer demographic data and purchasing patterns** for XYZ Retail using **Microsoft Excel** to uncover insights related to customer segmentation, product performance, and sales trends.

The analysis focuses on transforming raw transactional data into **meaningful business insights** through data cleaning, visualization, statistical analysis, and trend evaluation.

## Business Problem
XYZ Retail lacks a clear understanding of how customer demographics such as age, gender, and region influence purchasing behavior. Without structured analysis, it is difficult to design targeted marketing campaigns, forecast demand, and optimize product assortment.

---

## Business Objectives
- Identify key **customer segments** based on demographics and purchase behavior  
- Analyze **product category performance** and revenue contribution  
- Understand **regional and demographic buying patterns**  
- Identify **sales trends** to support forecasting and inventory decisions  
- Provide **data-driven insights** to optimize marketing strategies

---

## Analysis Methodology

### 1️⃣ Data Cleaning & Preparation
- Formatted datasets for readability and consistency  
- Standardized demographic and categorical fields  
- Ensured accurate numerical and date formatting  

---

### 2️⃣ Demographic Analysis
- Created an **age distribution histogram** to identify dominant customer age groups  
- Used pie charts to analyze **regional customer distribution**  

---

### 3️⃣ Purchase Behavior Analysis
- Analyzed purchase amount variability across product categories using **box plots**  
- Compared total purchases and revenue contribution using **bar and donut charts**  

---

### 4️⃣ Regional & Gender-Based Revenue Analysis
- Built **stacked bar charts** to analyze revenue by region segmented by gender  
- Created **relative stacked bar charts** to compare age group contributions across regions  

---

### 5️⃣ Trend & Time-Series Analysis
- Used **stacked area charts** to analyze monthly revenue trends by product category  
- Created line charts to identify **peak purchasing periods**  

---

### 6️⃣ Correlation & Statistical Analysis
- Analyzed the relationship between **customer age and purchase amount** using scatter plots  
- Identified spending patterns and potential correlations  

---

## Key Insights
- Certain age groups contribute disproportionately to total revenue  
- Product category performance varies significantly across regions  
- Some regions show strong seasonal purchasing trends  
- Higher purchase amounts are concentrated within specific demographic segments  

---

## Business Recommendations
- Design **targeted marketing campaigns** for high-value customer segments  
- Optimize inventory planning based on regional and demographic demand patterns  
- Focus promotions on top-performing product categories  
- Use trend insights to improve sales forecasting and stock management
